STRATEGIC OVERVIEW:


The Syrian region is in a state of open conflict. REDFOR launched coordinated and lethal attacks, leaving BLUFOR on the verge of retreat. Frontline is dyanamic, each coalition is seeking total dominance of the theatre.


THEATRE CONDITIONS:

Fog of War is active for each coalition and for all forces. Units will only see areas and units discovered by their own coalition's assets. The F10 map gives real-time information of the theatre, and therefore requires active reconnaissance to populate.

Supplies, weapons and aircraft and finite, for REDFOR and BLUFOR. All assets rely on supply deliveries (requested via F10 menu, or periodically). Losing supplies can severely affect the coalition.

Medium to long-range SAM networks are active across the region. These threats persist regardless of the frontline's position.
AI tasking relies on functionnal COMMS Antennas, the loss of these assets will degrade AI tasking.


MISSION OBJECTIVE:
As a pilot, your objective is to actively engage and neutralize all REDFOR targets and areas, by any means possible.

EXECUTION: 
Your priority is to identify and destroy enemy units or reinforce coalition sectors. Use reconnaissance tasks to locate hidden enemy forces.

Once a sector is neutralized, helicopter pilots are authorized to deploy for immediate capture or to escort AI capture helicopters moving forward from logistics areas.

Logistics management is essential for this operation. C-130J-30 aircraft are tasked with airdropping supplies to friendly airbases to deliver equipment and aircraft. Helicopter and C130J-30 crews are able to deploy and task ground units SAMs and FARPs.

Use resources wisely. Good hits and completed operations earn XP and supplies. Use these to call in resupply or AI tasks from within the F10 radio menu.

Good luck!
